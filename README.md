## Night Has Come

"Night Has Come" is a Korean mystery drama about students who go on a school trip and are suddenly forced to play a deadly mafia game. No one knows who to trust.

---

## What It's About
> A school trip turns into a nightmare when students are forced to play a killing game, just like the game Mafia.

---

## Reasons to Watch
1. The story is full of suspense  
2. You won’t guess what happens next  
3. It shows how fear changes people  
4. Every episode ends with a twist

---

## What I Learned
- People can change when they are scared  
- Trust is easy to break  
- Survival shows your true self
